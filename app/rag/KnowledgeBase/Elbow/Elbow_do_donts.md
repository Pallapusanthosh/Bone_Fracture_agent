## Do's and Don'ts for Elbow Fracture

### Do's

- Immobilize arm immediately
- Apply ice (15–20 mins)
- Keep arm elevated
- Follow doctor instructions
- Attend follow-ups

---

### Don'ts

- Do not move elbow forcefully
- Do not ignore numbness or swelling
- Do not delay treatment

---

### Emergency Warning

- Severe deformity
- Bone visible
- Loss of movement or pulse

---

### Key Insight

Early treatment prevents permanent stiffness and nerve damage.
