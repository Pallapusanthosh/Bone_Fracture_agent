## Treatment of Elbow Fractures

### 1. Non-Surgical Treatment

Used for stable fractures:

- Splint or cast (3–6 weeks)
- Immobilization at 90° elbow flexion
- Pain management

---

### 2. Closed Reduction

- Manual alignment of bones
- Used before casting

---

### 3. Surgical Treatment

Required when:

- Fracture is displaced
- Neurovascular injury present
- Open fracture

---

#### Surgical Methods:

- Pinning (K-wires)
- ORIF (plates, screws)
- External fixation (severe cases)

---

### Special Case: Olecranon Fracture

- Sling or splint if stable
- Surgery if displaced

---

### Key Insight

Early alignment + immobilization prevents deformity.
