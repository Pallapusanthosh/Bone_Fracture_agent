## Complications of Elbow Fractures

### Common Complications

- Joint stiffness
- Reduced range of motion
- Malunion (wrong healing)

---

### Serious Complications

- Nerve injury (median, radial, ulnar)
- Brachial artery injury
- Compartment syndrome

---

### Long-Term Issues

- Deformity (cubitus varus / valgus)
- Chronic pain
- Functional loss

---

### Key Insight

Neurovascular injury is the most dangerous complication.
