## Elbow Fracture Symptoms

### Common Symptoms

- Sudden severe pain in elbow and forearm
- Swelling around the joint
- Bruising
- Tenderness
- Inability to move or straighten the arm
- Pain during rotation (turning palm up/down)

---

### Functional Signs

- Difficulty bending or extending elbow
- Reduced range of motion
- Pain during supination/pronation

---

### Neurological Signs (Important)

- Numbness in hand or fingers
- Weak grip (nerve involvement)

---

### Severe / Emergency Signs

- Visible deformity (abnormal angle)
- Bone protruding through skin (open fracture)
- Loss of pulse or pale hand (vascular injury)

---

### Key Insight

Pain + inability to extend elbow = strong fracture indicator.
