## Causes of Elbow Fractures

### Common Causes

- Fall on outstretched hand (FOOSH) → most common
- Direct fall on elbow
- Direct blow (sports injury, accidents)

---

### High-Risk Activities

- Playground falls (children)
- Sports (football, gymnastics)
- Cycling / skateboarding accidents

---

### Mechanisms

- Hyperextension of elbow
- Axial loading (force transmitted through arm)
- Direct trauma

---

### Key Insight

FOOSH (fall on outstretched hand) is the most common mechanism.
