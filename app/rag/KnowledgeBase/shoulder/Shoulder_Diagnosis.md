## Diagnosis of Shoulder Fractures

### Clinical Examination

A healthcare provider will check:
- Pain and tenderness
- Swelling and deformity
- Range of motion
- Visible abnormalities

---

### Imaging Techniques

#### 1. X-ray (Primary test)
- Confirms presence of fracture
- Shows bone alignment and displacement

#### 2. CT Scan
- Provides detailed view of bone structure
- Used for complex fractures

#### 3. MRI
- Evaluates soft tissues (ligaments, tendons, cartilage)
- Helps identify associated injuries

---

### Fracture Classification in Diagnosis

#### Displaced Fracture
- Bone fragments have moved apart

#### Non-Displaced Fracture
- Bone remains aligned despite fracture

---

### Key Insight
Imaging is essential to determine severity and guide treatment decisions.
