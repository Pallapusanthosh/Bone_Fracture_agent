## Do's and Don'ts for Shoulder Fracture

### Do's

- Use a sling or immobilizer as advised
- Apply ice to reduce swelling
- Take prescribed medications
- Attend follow-up appointments
- Perform physiotherapy exercises when recommended

---

### Don'ts

- Do not move the shoulder forcefully
- Do not lift heavy objects
- Do not ignore severe pain or swelling
- Do not attempt to realign the shoulder yourself

---

### Emergency Situations

Seek immediate medical care if:
- Bone is visible through skin
- Severe deformity is present
- Complete inability to move the arm
- Intense, worsening pain

---

### Key Insight
Proper care during early stages prevents complications and improves recovery.
