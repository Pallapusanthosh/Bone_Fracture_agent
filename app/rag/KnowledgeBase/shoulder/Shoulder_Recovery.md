## Recovery and Rehabilitation

### Healing Time

Recovery depends on:
- Type of fracture
- Severity
- Treatment method

Typical duration:
- Few weeks to several months

---

### Rehabilitation

- Physical therapy is essential
- Helps restore:
  - Strength
  - Mobility
  - Range of motion

---

### Movement Guidelines

- Early immobilization is required
- Gradual movement is introduced later
- Avoid heavy lifting until fully healed

---

### Long-Term Outcomes

- Most patients regain function
- Some may experience:
  - Stiffness
  - Reduced range of motion

---

### Key Insight
Recovery is gradual and requires consistent rehabilitation for best outcomes.
