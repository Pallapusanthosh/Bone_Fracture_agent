## Shoulder Fracture Symptoms

Common symptoms of a shoulder fracture include:

- Pain in the shoulder (often severe)
- Swelling around the joint
- Bruising or discoloration
- Inability to move or use the shoulder normally
- Grinding or cracking sensation (crepitus) when moving the shoulder
- Visible deformity ("shoulder does not look right")
- Bump or abnormal shape due to displaced bone

### Additional specific signs:
- Limited range of motion
- Severe pain on movement
- Arm may feel weak or "dead"

### Emergency signs:
- Bone visible through skin (open fracture)
- Extreme swelling and deformity
- Complete inability to move the arm
