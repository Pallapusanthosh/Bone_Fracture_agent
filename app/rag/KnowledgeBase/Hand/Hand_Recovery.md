## Recovery and Rehabilitation

### Healing Time

- 3–6 weeks (basic healing)
- Full recovery: 6–8 weeks or more

---

### Rehabilitation

- Hand therapy / occupational therapy
- Improves:
  - Strength
  - Flexibility
  - Grip function

---

### Movement Guidelines

- Early controlled motion is important
- Avoid long immobilization (>3 weeks) to prevent stiffness

---

### Key Insight

Hand function depends heavily on early mobilization after stabilization.
